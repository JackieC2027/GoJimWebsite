Hack Squat

// gif of demostration

// tips on form in a organized list

  1. Stand with your feet shoulder-width apart on the platform and your back against the pad.
  2. Place your hands on the handles and release the safety bar.
  3. Bend your knees and lower your body, keeping your back straight and your chest up.
  4. Lower yourself until your thighs are parallel to the platform or lower.
  5. Push yourself back up to the starting position by straightening your legs, exhaling as you lift.
  6. Repeat for the desired number of reps.
  7. Maintain proper form throughout the exercise, keeping your knees in line with your toes and avoiding rounding your back.
  8. Increase the weight gradually over time as you get stronger.

Barbell Squats

  1. Stand with your feet shoulder-width apart and position the barbell across your upper back, just below your neck.
  2. Grasp the bar with an overhand grip, keeping your elbows down and closeto your body.
  3. Bend your knees and lower your body, keeping your back straight and your chest up.
  4. Lower yourself until your thighs are parallel to the ground or lower.
  5. Push yourself back up to the starting position by straightening your legs, exhaling as you lift.
  6. Repeat for the desired number of reps.
  7. Maintain proper form throughout the exercise, keeping your knees in line with your toes and your back straight.
  8. Use a spotter or safety rails if necessary, especially when lifting heavy weights.
  9. Increase the weight gradually over time as you get stronger.

Leg Extensions

  1. Adjust the leg extension machine to fit your height and sit on the seat with your back against the pad.
  2. Position your legs under the padded bar, just above your feet.
  3. Grasp the handles and release the safety bar.
  4. Extend your legs, lifting the padded bar with your feet, exhaling as you lift.
  5. Pause briefly at the top of the movement, then slowly release the bar back to the starting position, inhaling as you lower.
  6. Repeat for the desired number of reps.
  7. Maintain proper form throughout the exercise, keeping your back against the pad and your feet in line with your knees.
  8. Increase the weight gradually over time as you get stronger.
  9. Use a spotter or safety rails if necessary, especially when lifting heavy weights.

Leg Press

  1. Sit on the leg press machine with your back against the pad and your feet on the platform.
  2. Adjust the seat and foot position to align your knees with the pivot point of the machine.
  3. Grasp the handles and release the safety bar.
  4. Press the platform away from you by extending your legs, exhaling as you lift.
  5. Lower the platform back down to the starting position by bending your knees, inhaling as you lower.
  6. Repeat for the desired number of reps.
  7. Maintain proper form throughout the exercise, keeping your back against the pad and your feet flat on the platform.
  8. Increase the weight gradually over time as you get stronger.
  9. Use a spotter or safety rails if necessary, especially when lifting heavy weights.
